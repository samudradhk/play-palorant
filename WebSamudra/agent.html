OMUN
Omun is a Controller agent who manipulates shadows to disrupt enemies. Shrouded Step allows him to teleport short distances, Paranoia sends out a shadow wave that blinds enemies, Dark Cover creates a smoke orb to block vision, and From the Shadows is his ultimate ability that allows Omen to teleport to any location on the map, becoming a shadow during the process.

BRAMS
Brams is a Controller agent who supports the team with area control. Sky Smoke summons smoke clouds to block vision, Stim Beacon increases the firing speed of nearby allies, Incendiary fires a molotov grenade for area damage, and Orbital Strike is the ultimate ability that calls down an orbital strike dealing massive damage in a specific area.

RAZI
Razi is a Duelist agent who relies on explosions and area damage. Boom Bot deploys a small robot that chases enemies and explodes, Blast Pack is an explosive that can be thrown and detonated, Paint Shells is a grenade that explodes into smaller fragments, and Showstopper is an ultimate rocket launcher that deals massive damage in a wide area.

SKAY
Skay is an Initiator agent who supports the team with healing and disruption. Regrowth heals allies, Trailblazer summons a tiger that stuns enemies, Guiding Light releases a hawk that blinds enemies, and Seekers track enemies to reveal their locations.

VORU
Voru is a Duelist agent focused on camouflage and confusing enemies. Fakeout creates fake footsteps to deceive enemies, Blindside throws a flashbang that blinds enemies, Gatecrash projects teleportation to a designated location, and Dimensional Drift allows Yoru to move in another dimension, making him invisible and temporarily invulnerable.
